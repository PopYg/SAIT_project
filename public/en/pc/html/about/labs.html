Global Labs

SAIT founded as Samsung Group's R&D Hub

SAIT collaborates with talented people around the world to keep abreast of state-of-the-art technologies, drive new trends, and
create opportunities. Through Open Innovation, SAIT's global labs located in Yokohama, Beijing, San Jose, Boston, Moscow, Bengaluru,
London, Frankfurt collaborate with regional universities, labs, and companies to create better technologies for all.
