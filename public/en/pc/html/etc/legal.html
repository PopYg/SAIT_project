Privacy Statement

The Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology

Information Collected

You can visit this web site without providing us with your personal information. Our web server collects the domain names, not the e-mail address itself, of visitors. Our web server also seeks (as many Web sites do) to place a "cookie" (a small data file) on your computer's hard drive disk, which allows the server to recognize the computer when it connects to our web server again, in order to enhance your web experience. This cookie is not used to obtain your name or any other personal data, and the information obtained is used only for internal purposes, such as to improve site navigation and to measure the effectiveness of our web site promotion. However, if you prefer not to accept cookies, you may set your browser to reject them or to alert you before it is installed.

In addition, there are portions of this Web site where we may need to collect personal information from you for a specific purpose, such as to provide you with certain information you request. The information collected from you may include your name, address, telephone/fax number, e-mail address, or IP address (an IP address identifies the type of browser you are using, such as Netscape or Internet Explorer, by assigning a unique number) for general system administration to serve you better by diagnosing problems with our server.

This Site is not intended for persons under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from or about children, and we do not knowingly market our products or services to children.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not disclose, sell, trade, or rent Personal Information without your prior consent, except to the extent necessary to provide you the material or service you have requested. In certain rare circumstances, we may be compelled by law to disclose your Personal Information, such as in connection with a lawsuit or other legal proceedings. In the event disclosure is required, we will use reasonable efforts to try to secure confidential treatment of the disclosed Personal Information, including prohibiting use of the information for any commercial purpose.

Viewing and Editing Personal Information

You may view or change your personal information saved on our web server as often as you require. To change or view your personal information click on Change Personal Information for immediate access to your information or contact an officer in charge to request for changes. Once you request for changes in your personal information we do not use the information or provide them to 3rd party until the information has been corrected.

You may cancel the agreement on use, collection, and provision of your personal information at any time. If you would like to cancel the agreement please contact us by email or postal service, or telephone for an instant cancellation.
